Our team think that the best solution for Mohammed's problem is to create an e-commerce web
application using react for great ux and ui containing two parts : one for the administrator
and the other for clients.

the basic functionnalities of this application are :
    First Part :
	- show products, prices, rate, sizes, descriptions, cathegories, feedbacks.
	- sign in & sign up for new users.
	- select cathegories.
	- select a product.
	- put a product in the shopping cart.
	- validate a cart.
	- buy one product.
	- communicate with the administrator.
	- rate products.
	- explore and modify on the shopping cart 

     Second Part :
	- add a product(description, price, title, quantity...)
	- modify a product
	- delete a product
	- explore statistics
	- explore clients
	- validate transactions.
	


la conception de notre application est faite à l'aide du logiciel Figma qui permet 
la création d'un prototype facilitant la bien comprehension des fonctionnalités et 
d'éviter les mal entendus entre client et developpeurs
